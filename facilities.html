Facilities

Sutuscourt is an elegant budget hotel located at Soi Bua Khao in the central area of Pattaya. We offer over 300 rooms with 5 different types, featuring stunning views of Pattaya. Our convenient location allows easy access to various places such as shopping malls (Central Royal Garden, Mike Shopping Mall, Carrefour), Seven Eleven, hospitals, and nightlife attractions. The sea is just 800 meters away, and you can easily reach it by walking or taking a taxi.

Sutuscourt is a perfect resting place for couples, friends, and families who seek relaxation in a private atmosphere. We offer daily and monthly stays, and we have ample parking space for bikes and cars, including a ground floor car park.
